My Products

25 yrs ago, I received a set of champagne flutes that were engraved with our names on them. Now 25 yrs later, I still cherish them! I strive to create that same cherished keepsake for my customers so let me create for you a personalized keepsake that you will love for years to come!

All of my custom glassware is hand painted with an acrylic paint and waterproofed with varnish to preserve the design. To clean, just gently hand wash and enjoy with your favorite wine!

I also paint wall murals and windows! I can create a product themed scene for your business or even create a Holiday scene for your front windows at home!

A Sipping Pretty History

As a little girl, I have always loved to draw, hey I could draw the little turtle on the matchbook cover (now Im dating myself) Drawing was always a hobby of mine, but one that I put on the back burner of my life as I raised my family. Until several years ago. My friends and I had gone to a wine tasting. While I was there, I purchase a pretty wine glass painted with little cherry blossoms on it. When I got home and went to put it away, I noticed I had the same plain wine glasses in my china cabinet. At that moment, I turned to my husband and said, "I can paint. I can make glasses just like those!" So, out came the paint & the brushes and I started painting everything! Wine glasses, bottles, dishes etc. It was my quiet little zen moment for myself.

A year later (2016), my twin nieces were celebrating their Sweet 16. Having always loved unique personalized gifts, I made them each a champagne glass that resembled their dresses (the tulle skirt and pearl beading, etc) After that moment, Sipping Pretty Designs by Helen was born!! I took my little zen hobby and opened an Etsy account and started making Wedding Toasting Glasses. Recreating each couples personal unique wedding style! In the summer of 2017, The owner of the Junction Boutique of Belford, NJ called me asking me if I would like to join the Junction Boutique family. With her invite, came a new sense of creativity that I never knew I had!! Once again the wine glasses came out and so did the ideas of painted Mermaids, Birthday Glasses, Beach scenes, weddings and more! Amazing, it all started with a cherry blossom wine glass!
